About Me

Rosmaël Zidane received his Master’s degree in Science and Technology from the University of Dschang, Cameroon. With a strong passion for modern computing, he joined the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), where he completed a structured Master’s program in Data Science, focusing on deep learning. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Trento, Italy. His research focuses on AI-driven vulnerability detection in source code, leveraging graph-based models and domain knowledge integration. He is passionate about artificial intelligence, software security, and innovative data-driven methodologies to improve the reliability of modern software systems.

Research Interests

  • AI-based vulnerability detection in source code
  • Graph neural networks and software security
  • Mining software repositories for threat analysis
  • Machine learning for cybersecurity applications
  • Knowledge graph integration for software analysis
