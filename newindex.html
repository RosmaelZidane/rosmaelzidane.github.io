About Me

Rosmael Zidane received his Master’s degree in Science and Technology from the University of Dschang, Cameroon. With a strong interest in modern science, he was admitted to the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences, where he completed a structured Master's program in Data Science, focusing on deep learning. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Trento. His research is centered on AI-driven vulnerability detection in source code, leveraging graph-based models and domain knowledge integration. Passionate about artificial intelligence and scientific innovation, he is dedicated to developing advanced techniques for improving software security.
